HOW TO MAKE VIETNAMESE SAVORY MINI PANCAKES (BANH KHOT)

Posted by Hana Nguyen on Feb 03, 2021

What Is Banh Khot?

Vietnamese savory mini pancakes ( Banh Khot), is made from a batter of rice flour, corn starch, tumeric powder and coconut milk. The batter is fried on a cast iron or Aebleskiver frying pan to crispy perfection on the outside and fluffy on the inside.These mini pancakes are topped with shrimp, pork and green onion. They come with a plate stacked high in Vietnamese herbs and a small bowl of spicy and sweet dipping sauce.

The premade Banh Khot flour mix are sold in many Asian supermarkets alongside all the other small bags of premade flour mixes. In Vietnam, you can find Banh Khot sold in fancy restaurants or right out on the streets on rollaway carts.

How to cook Banh Khot

INGREDIENTS

• 25 large shrimp (peeled and deveined)
• 5 green onions (slice thinly and separate the greens from the whites)
• 150 grams ground pork
• 1/2 teaspoon salt
• 1 premade Banh Khot flour mix
• 1 cup coconut milk
• Vegetable oil
• Vietnamese dipping sauce
• Assorted greens: Lettuce, Mustard Greens, Vietnamese Coriander, Sorrell, Perilla, and/or Mint
• Banh Khot/Aebleskiver frying pan

Processing Banh Khot

In a small bowl, combine the shrimp, pork, whites of green onions, garlic, salt and pepper. Marinate for at least 30 minutes.Whisk together all the batter ingredients.

• Heat the Banh Khot pan on high and add about 1/2 teaspoon of vegetable oil to each well. Make sure to coat the oil all over the inside of the wells.
• Add about 1 tablespoon of batter to each well.
• Quickly add shrimp and pork on top of the batter.
• Place a lid on the pan and let the batter cook for about 3-4 minutes.
• Remove the lid and let it continue cooking for another minute without the lid.
• Remove the Banh Khot from the pan with a spoon, starting from the edges and scooping inward.
If the batter breaks apart or stick to the pan, it might not be done yet. Continue to let the batter cook until crispy on the outside before trying to remove again.

To Serve

Serve with your favorite Vietnamese herbs and a bowl of sweet and spicy dipping sauce.
